US CPI drops 0.4% in June, biggest monthly decline in six years; core inflation eases to 2.6% y/y
美国6月CPI环比下降0.4%，创六年来最大跌幅；核心通胀同比降至2.6%
The US Labor Department reported June CPI fell 0.4% month-over-month and rose 3.5% year-over-year, both below expectations. Traders slashed July Fed hike bets to roughly 15% from over 40%.
