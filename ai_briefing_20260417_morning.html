China's five-ministry interim rules on AI humanized interaction services take effect July 15, ban virtual intimacy for minors
2026年4月10日，国家互联网信息办公室、国家发改委、工信部、公安部、市场监管总局联合发布《人工智能拟人化互动服务管理暂行办法》（第21号令），将于7月15日起施行。该办法适用于向中国境内公众提供持续性情感互动的 AI 服务（如 AI 伴侣、虚拟人格），不适用于智能客服、知识问答等功能性工具。核心管理要求包括：禁止向未成年人提供虚拟亲密关系服务；设置未成年人模式和限制；强制 AI 标识披露；上线前须完成安全评估；用户数超百万须定期报备。违规者面临 1 万至 20 万元罚款及暂停服务。
On April 10, 2026, China's CAC, NDRC, MIIT, MPS, and SAMR jointly issued Interim Measures on AI Humanized Interaction Services (Order No. 21), effective July 15. The rules cover AI services providing sustained emotional interaction (e.g., AI companions, virtual personas), but explicitly exempt functional tools like customer service and Q&A bots. Key requirements: ban on providing virtual intimate relationships to minors; mandatory AI disclosure labels; pre-launch safety assessments; periodic reporting for services exceeding 1 million users.
