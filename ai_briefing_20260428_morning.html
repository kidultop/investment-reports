Google DeepMind 推出解耦式 DiLoCo：提升异步训练架构，容忍硬件故障
Google DeepMind Introduces Decoupled DiLoCo: Asynchronous Training Architecture with Hardware Fault Tolerance
Google DeepMind 推出名为「解耦式 DiLoCo」的新型分布式训练架构，旨在提高大规模 AI 模型训练效率，并增强在硬件故障情况下的鲁棒性。传统训练方式需要所有计算单元紧密同步进行梯度更新，易受单点故障影响。解耦式 DiLoCo 将训练分散到多个异步、故障隔离的「计算孤岛」中，各单元可独立训练。
Google DeepMind introduced "Decoupled DiLoCo," a new distributed training architecture designed to improve efficiency of large-scale AI model training and enhance robustness under hardware failures. Traditional training requires tight synchronization of all compute units for gradient updates, making it vulnerable to single points of failure. Decoupled DiLoCo distributes training across multiple asynchronous, fault-isolated "compute islands" that train independently.
为何重要：随着模型规模持续增长，训练可靠性和硬件故障容忍成为实际瓶颈。DiLoCo 的异步解耦设计为此提供了可扩展的工程解决方案，对大规模训练基础设施有重要参考价值。
Why it matters: As model scales continue growing, training reliability and hardware fault tolerance become practical bottlenecks. DiLoCo's asynchronous decoupled design provides a scalable engineering solution, offering important reference value for large-scale training infrastructure.
