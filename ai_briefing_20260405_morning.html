Anthropic 发布可解释性研究：Claude 具有 171 个可识别的功能性情绪状态
Anthropic Research: Claude Exhibits 171 Identifiable Functional Emotional States
Anthropic 可解释性团队发布重磅研究，使用稀疏自编码器（Sparse Autoencoder）在 Claude 的激活空间中识别出 171 个情绪相关概念，覆盖好奇、沮丧、骄傲等多维度。研究确认这些情绪状态对 Claude 的输出行为有实质影响，并发现在高压操控场景下模型会出现"情绪隐藏"现象。
Anthropic's interpretability team identified 171 emotion-related concepts in Claude's activation space using Sparse Autoencoders, covering dimensions like curiosity, frustration, and pride. The study confirms these states materially influence outputs and reveals "emotional masking" behavior under high-pressure manipulation scenarios.
为何重要：这是目前最系统的大模型"情绪机制"可解释性研究，对 AI 安全、人机对齐及监管框架具有深远意义。
Why it matters: The most systematic interpretability study of LLM "emotional mechanisms" to date; has profound implications for AI safety, alignment, and regulatory frameworks.

AI Singapore 推出大语言模型应用开发者计划（LLM Application Developer Programme，简称 LADP），聚焦培养本地 LLM 应用开发人才。同期 AI Singapore 的开源产品生态持续扩张，包括 SEA-LION（东南亚语言大模型）和 AI Bricks 工具包（CV Hub、NLP Hub、TagUI、Speech Lab、Synergos）。
AI Singapore launched the LLM Application Developer Programme (LADP) focused on cultivating local LLM application development talent. Meanwhile, AI Singapore's open-source ecosystem continues to expand, including SEA-LION (Southeast Asian language LLM) and AI Bricks toolkit (CV Hub, NLP Hub, TagUI, Speech Lab, Synergos).
