OpenAI 登陆 AWS，终结微软独家云合作 — $500 亿合作曝光
OpenAI Lands on AWS, Ending Microsoft's Exclusive Cloud Partnership — $50B Deal Revealed
4 月 28 日，AWS 与 OpenAI 联合宣布重大合作：GPT-5.5、Codex 及 Bedrock Managed Agents 正式在 Amazon Bedrock 上提供预览。据路透社报道，合作条款包括 Amazon 对 OpenAI 投资 500 亿美元、OpenAI 承诺在 8 年内向 AWS 投入 1000 亿美元算力支出。此前一天（4/27），微软官方宣布与 OpenAI 的合作转为非独家协议，有效期至 2032 年。这意味着 OpenAI 在一周内完成了从"微软专属"到"全面多云"的战略转身。
On April 28, AWS and OpenAI jointly announced a landmark partnership: GPT-5.5, Codex, and Bedrock Managed Agents are now available in preview on Amazon Bedrock. Reuters reports the deal includes a $50B Amazon investment in OpenAI and a $100B OpenAI commitment to AWS over 8 years. The day prior (Apr 27), Microsoft announced its partnership with OpenAI had transitioned to a non-exclusive agreement through 2032. Within a single week, OpenAI pivoted from "Microsoft-exclusive" to "fully multi-cloud."
为何重要：这是云计算史上最具颠覆性的格局变化之一。OpenAI 不再绑定任何单一云厂商，企业客户可在 Azure、AWS 及 Google Cloud 之间自由选择。对行业而言，这意味着模型厂商不再依赖云平台的分发渠道，AI 基础设施的竞争从"谁能独家拿到最好模型"转向"谁能提供最好的算力和部署服务"。
Why it matters:This is one of the most disruptive shifts in cloud computing history. OpenAI is no longer bound to any single cloud provider, giving enterprise customers freedom to choose between Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud. The industry is shifting from "who can exclusively offer the best models" to "who provides the best compute and deployment services."

Google 宣布 400 亿美元投资 Anthropic，AI 产业最大单笔交易
Google Commits Up to $40B Investment in Anthropic, Largest AI Deal to Date
4 月 24 日，Google 母公司 Alphabet 宣布对 Anthropic 进行最高 400 亿美元战略投资：100 亿美元现金立即到账，按 Anthropic 3800 亿美元估值入股，剩余 300 亿美元视业绩目标追加。该交易的核心逻辑在于算力绑定——Anthropic 获得资金后大量采购 Google TPU 算力服务，形成了"Google 给钱 → Anthropic 买算力 → 资金回流 Google"的闭环。
On April 24, Google's parent Alphabet announced a strategic investment of up to $40B in Anthropic: $10B cash upfront at a $380B valuation, with $30B conditional on performance milestones. The deal's core logic is compute lock-in — Anthropic will use the funding to purchase Google's TPU cloud services, creating a circular flow of capital from Google to Anthropic and back to Google.
为何重要：Anthropic 的估值（3800 亿美元）已逼近 OpenAI（8520 亿美元），Claude 年化收入据报从约 10 亿美元暴涨至 300 亿美元。Google 通过这笔交易同时实现了"投资顶级 AI 公司 + 确保大客户采购自家 TPU 算力"的双重目标，堪称 AI 时代最具战略性的投资之一。
Why it matters:Anthropic's $380B valuation is approaching OpenAI's $852B, with Claude's annualized revenue reportedly surging from ~$1B to $30B. Google achieves a dual objective: investing in a top AI company while locking in a major TPU cloud customer. It's arguably the most strategically designed investment of the AI era.

China Officially Blocks Meta's $2B Acquisition of Manus, AI Talent Declared Strategic Asset
4 月 27 日，中国国家发改委正式否决了 Meta 对中国背景 AI 创业公司 Manus 的 20 亿美元收购案，并要求双方在数周内完成交易撤回。监管机构明确将 AI 人才、数据及知识产权列为战略资产，跨境转移需国家审批。Manus 虽已迁册新加坡，但因核心团队及技术源自中国，仍被纳入监管范围。
On April 27, China's NDRC officially blocked Meta's $2B acquisition of Chinese-founded AI startup Manus, ordering the parties to unwind the deal within weeks. Regulators explicitly classified AI talent, data, and IP as strategic assets requiring state approval for cross-border transfer. Although Manus relocated to Singapore, its core team and technology origins in China kept it within regulatory scope.
为何重要：这是中美科技博弈在 AI 领域的标志性事件。此先例意味着所有涉及中国 AI 人才或技术的外资收购都将面临严格审查，即便公司注册地在海外。这对跨国 AI 投资格局产生深远影响，新加坡作为"中立地带"的定位也受到挑战。
Why it matters:This is a landmark event in US-China tech rivalry. The precedent means all foreign acquisitions involving Chinese AI talent or technology will face strict scrutiny, even if the company is registered overseas. It profoundly reshapes cross-border AI investment and challenges Singapore's positioning as a "neutral ground."
