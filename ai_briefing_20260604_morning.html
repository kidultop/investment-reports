OpenAI 正式成立机器人部门，时隔六年重返物理世界
OpenAI Establishes Robotics Division, Returns to Physical World After Six Years
US
6月1日，CEO Sam Altman 在 X 发布招聘帖，联合创始人 Greg Brockman 同步宣布 OpenAI Robotics 团队正式成立。团队由 VP of Research Aditya Ramesh（DALL·E / Sora 核心开发者）领导，从内部 Worldsim 世界模拟项目演变而来。短期目标：开发辅助技工建设基础设施的机器人；长期愿景：让每个人拥有个人机器人。OpenAI 曾于 2016-2019 年探索机器人（推出 OpenAI Gym、Dactyl 机械手），2020 年解散团队转向 LLM。2025 年 2 月与 Figure AI 的技术路线分歧终结合作后，OpenAI 选择自建硬件。招聘覆盖全栈硬件、运营、系统及机器学习工程师，目前未公布具体产品或时间表。
On June 1, CEO Sam Altman posted recruitment on X, with co-founder Greg Brockman announcing the official formation of OpenAI Robotics. Led by VP of Research Aditya Ramesh (creator of DALL·E and Sora), the team evolved from the internal Worldsim world-simulation project. Short-term goal: robots assisting skilled workers in infrastructure construction. Long-term vision: personal robots for everyone. OpenAI previously explored robotics from 2016-2019 (OpenAI Gym, Dactyl robotic hand), disbanded in 2020 to focus on LLMs. After ending the Figure AI partnership in February 2025 over technical route disagreements, OpenAI chose to build hardware in-house. No product timeline or partners announced yet.
为何重要：OpenAI 从纯软件模型公司向软硬件一体转型，与 Tesla Bot、Figure AI、1X 等机器人公司直接竞争。由 Sora/DALL·E 核心开发者领导意味着世界模型能力将直接赋能机器人，Sora 的物理世界理解可能是关键差异化优势。这也为 OpenAI 预期 9 月 IPO 增加了新的增长故事。
Why it matters:OpenAI pivots from pure-software models to integrated hardware+software, competing directly with Tesla Bot, Figure AI, and 1X. Led by the creator of Sora/DALL·E, world model capabilities will directly power robotics — Sora's physical world understanding could be a key differentiator. This also adds a new growth narrative for the anticipated September IPO.

MiniMax M3 发布：SWE-Bench Pro 超越 GPT-5.5，开源三合一旗舰
MiniMax M3 Launch: Surpasses GPT-5.5 on SWE-Bench Pro, Open-Source Three-in-One Flagship
CN
6月1日，MiniMax 正式发布并开源新一代旗舰模型 MiniMax M3。采用自研稀疏注意力架构 MSA（MiniMax Sparse Attention），在 SWE-Bench Pro 上小幅超越 GPT-5.5 和 Gemini 3.1 Pro，接近 Claude Opus 4.7，在 Claw-Eval（端到端 Agent 评测）中达到开源最优。M3 是当前开源模型中唯一同时具备前沿 Coding 能力、1M 超长上下文和原生多模态（文本/图片/视频输入 + 工具调用）三项能力的模型。训练仅用 12 小时无人干预，CUDA 内核加速达到 9.4 倍。此外 MiniMax 已启动科创板 IPO 辅导。
On June 1, MiniMax officially released and open-sourced its new flagship model MiniMax M3. Using a custom MiniMax Sparse Attention (MSA) architecture, it slightly surpasses GPT-5.5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro on SWE-Bench Pro, approaching Claude Opus 4.7, and achieves open-source best on Claw-Eval (end-to-end agent benchmark). M3 is the only open-source model combining frontier coding, 1M context, and native multimodality (text/image/video input + tool calling). Trained with only 12 hours of unattended training, achieving 9.4x CUDA kernel acceleration. MiniMax has also started STAR Market IPO counseling.
为何重要：中国开源模型在 Coding 和 Agent 能力上首次同时逼近或超越顶级闭源模型，且唯一实现 Coding + 长上下文 + 多模态三合一开源。12 小时无人工干预训练 + 9.4x CUDA 加速展示了极高的训练工程效率。科创板 IPO 启动意味着中国 AI 公司估值锚点从硅谷逻辑转向 A 股资本市场。
Why it matters:Chinese open-source models match or exceed top proprietary models on coding and agent capabilities for the first time, with the only three-in-one open-source model (coding + long context + multimodality). 12-hour unattended training with 9.4x CUDA acceleration demonstrates exceptional training engineering efficiency. The STAR Market IPO counseling signals Chinese AI companies anchoring valuations to A-share capital markets rather than Silicon Valley logic.

arXiv W22 精选：多 Agent 世界模型、多奖励 RL 与 Agent 安全框架
arXiv W22 Highlights: Multi-Agent World Models, Multi-Reward RL, and Agent Safety
Research
Gamma-World（357↑，本周最高票）：首个原生多 Agent 世界模型，突破双人限制，支持任意 N-Agent 在共享空间协作模拟——游戏 AI、具身仿真、自动驾驶训练的基础设施。DVAO：动态方差自适应优势优化，解决 GRPO 在多奖励场景下训练坍塌问题——对工业 RLHF/RLVR 部署的即插即用修复。AgentDoG 1.5：轻量可扩展的 Agent 安全对齐框架，从训练时对齐扩展到运行时实时行为对齐——企业级 Agent 部署的必要基础设施。LocateAnything：并行边界框解码，恢复边界框几何耦合结构，显著降低推理延迟。WBench：首个世界模型多轮交互评估基准，统一 5 维能力评测。Qwen-VLA：统一视觉-语言-动作建模，探索跨载体迁移能力。
Gamma-World (357↑, highest-voted): First native multi-agent world model breaking the two-player limit, supporting N-agent collaborative simulation in shared space — foundational infrastructure for game AI, embodied simulation, and autonomous driving training. DVAO: Dynamic variance-adaptive advantage optimization, solving GRPO training collapse in multi-reward scenarios — plug-and-play fix for industrial RLHF/RLVR deployments. AgentDoG 1.5: Lightweight scalable agent safety alignment framework extending from training-time to runtime real-time behavioral alignment — necessary infrastructure for enterprise agent deployment. LocateAnything: Parallel bounding box decoding restoring geometric coupling, significantly reducing inference latency. WBench: First multi-turn interactive evaluation benchmark for world models with 5-dimension unified assessment. Qwen-VLA: Unified vision-language-action modeling exploring cross-embodiment transfer.
为何重要：W22 整体信号是 AI 子系统从"研究 Demo"进入"可仿真、可调度、可部署"的工程化阶段：世界模型支持多 Agent 模拟，RL 后训练从通用算法转向专用优化，Agent 部署聚焦安全、跨载体兼容和高效感知。
Why it matters:The W22 signal is that AI subsystems are moving from "research demo" to engineering-grade "simulatable, schedulable, deployable" stage: world models support multi-agent simulation, RL post-training shifts to specialized optimizations, and agent deployment focuses on safety, cross-embodiment compatibility, and efficient perception.

新加坡为 15 万公务员建立 AI Agent 注册表
Singapore Building AI Agent Registry for 150,000 Public Officers
SG
6月2日，新加坡 GovTech 宣布正在开发AI Agent 注册表，作为更大套件 AI Assistant Desk 的核心组成部分。该注册表将追踪政府内所有 AI Agent 的所有者和活动，确保 15 万公务员在使用 AI 编程、生成报告、安排会议时的数据安全。目前超过一半的公务员已定期使用 Pair 聊天机器人。安全限制包括：Agent 不得删除文件或向外部收件人发邮件，内置自动化卫生检查器和 AI 驱动的持续渗透测试。此外 GovTech 还在 18 所学校试用 AI 批改助手 Mark.ly，在 10 所学校试用母语练习机器人 LangBuddy。
On June 2, Singapore GovTech announced development of an AI Agent Registry as a core component of the AI Assistant Desk suite. The registry will track owners and activities of all AI agents to ensure data security for 150,000 public officers using AI for coding, report generation, and meeting scheduling. Over half of all officers already regularly use the Pair chatbot. Security restrictions: agents cannot delete files or email external recipients, with built-in automated hygiene checkers and AI-driven continuous penetration testing. GovTech is also trialing AI marking assistant Mark.ly in 18 schools and mother tongue practice bot LangBuddy in 10 schools.
为何重要：新加坡是全球首个为政府 AI Agent 建立系统化注册和管控框架的国家。AI Assistant Desk 的安全分层设计（即使替换底层模型，安全规则不变）是一个重要的架构思想，确保从 Claude 切换到新模型时不会产生安全真空。这一模式可能成为其他政府部署 Agent 的参考范本。
Why it matters:Singapore is the first country to build a systematic registry and governance framework for government AI agents. The AI Assistant Desk's layered security design (security rules persist even when swapping underlying models) is an important architectural principle, ensuring no security vacuum when switching from Claude to a new model. This model may become a reference for other governments deploying agents.
