诺奖得主、AlphaFold 之父 John Jumper 离开 DeepMind 加盟 Anthropic
Nobel Laureate and AlphaFold Co-Creator John Jumper Leaves DeepMind for Anthropic
US
6月20日（周五），2024 年诺贝尔化学奖得主、Google DeepMind 副总裁 John Jumper 通过 X 平台公开宣布，将离开效力近 9 年的 DeepMind，加入竞争对手 Anthropic。Jumper 与 Demis Hassabis 共同开发了 AlphaFold 2——该模型已完成超 2 亿种蛋白质结构预测，2024 年为两人赢得诺贝尔化学奖。消息源透露，Jumper 并非近期唯一离开 DeepMind 的核心人物——报道称"不是唯一一个离开 DeepMind 的大人物"，暗示存在更广泛的核心人才外流。Hassabis 公开感谢了 Jumper 过去九年的"非凡合作"，称 AlphaFold"改变了世界"。Anthropic 在此之前已招揽 Transformer 共同发明者 Noam Shazeer（6月18日）的反向迁移，连续吸纳 Google 顶尖 AI 人才。
On Friday, June 20, 2024 Nobel Chemistry laureate and Google DeepMind VP John Jumper publicly announced via X that he is leaving DeepMind after nearly 9 years to join rival Anthropic. Jumper co-developed AlphaFold 2 with Demis Hassabis — a model that has predicted over 200 million protein structures and earned the pair the 2024 Nobel Prize. Sources indicate Jumper is "not the only big name" leaving DeepMind, suggesting a broader talent exodus. Hassabis publicly thanked Jumper for "an extraordinary collaboration" and said AlphaFold "changed the world." Anthropic had also recruited Transformer co-inventor Noam Shazeer (June 18) from Google, marking back-to-back major talent wins from DeepMind.
为何重要：这是 AI 行业历史上最高规格的人才流动——一位诺奖得主离开 Google 加入 Anthropic。Anthropic 短期内连获 Shazeer（架构）和 Jumper（科学计算）两员大将，科研实力急剧上升。DeepMind 在不到一周时间内接连失去两位核心人物，可能预示内部战略调整或路线分歧，对 Google 维持 AI 科学领导地位构成实质性挑战。
Why it matters:This is one of the highest-profile talent moves in AI history — a Nobel laureate leaving Google for Anthropic. Anthropic has now added both Shazeer (architecture) and Jumper (scientific computing) in one week, dramatically strengthening its research firepower. DeepMind losing two core figures in less than a week may signal internal strategy shifts or strategic disagreements, posing a real challenge to Google's AI scientific leadership.

Alibaba Qwen Launches Qwen-Robot Suite: First Complete Embodied AI Model Family
CN
6月16日，阿里巴巴千问团队重磅发布Qwen-Robot 系列——千问大模型家族首个完整的具身智能（Embodied AI）模型产品线，包含三大模型：Qwen-RobotManip（VLA 操作模型，赋予机器人灵巧的"手"）、Qwen-RobotNav（VLN 移动模型，赋予机器人认路的"脚"）、Qwen-RobotWorld（世界模型，赋予机器人会思考的"大脑"）。三大模型可单独部署也可协同运转，覆盖从工厂自动化到家庭服务等多种机器人形态。这是继 Qwen3.7-Plus 之后千问在 Physical AI 方向的重大延伸，标志着阿里从"数字世界对话"向"物理世界交互"扩张的战略意图。

Why it matters:Alibaba is the first Chinese major player to release a complete embodied AI model matrix covering hands-feet-brain full-stack. Compared to OpenAI's June robotics restart (team formation stage) and NVIDIA Cosmos 3's single-world-model approach, Qwen-Robot is more productized. Embodied AI is considered the "last mile" of AI — large models moving from screens to the physical world is a critical AGI milestone.

DeepMind 发布《从 AGI 到 ASI》报告，定义四条超智能演化路径
DeepMind Releases "From AGI to ASI" Report, Defining Four Pathways to Superintelligence
Research
6月12日，Google DeepMind 在官方研究页面发布重磅报告《From AGI to ASI》（arXiv: 2606.12683）。作者阵容包括 DeepMind 联合创始人兼首席 AGI 科学家 Shane Legg、Marcus Hutter（Universal AI 理论奠基人）、Thore Graepel、Joel Z. Leibo 等 15 位核心研究者。报告定义 ASI 为"在几乎所有任务上稳定超越大型人类专家集体协作的系统"，并提出 AGI 到 ASI 的四条潜在路径：1) 持续规模化 AGI；2) AI 范式跃迁；3) AI 递归自我改进；4) 来自大规模多智能体集体的涌现。报告警告：AGI 的引入可能并非"单一变革性事件"，而是"由 AI 驱动的科学突破引起的连续性变革"——这要求跨学科全球性准备。
On June 12, Google DeepMind published the landmark report "From AGI to ASI" (arXiv: 2606.12683) on its official research page. Authors include DeepMind co-founder and Chief AGI Scientist Shane Legg, Marcus Hutter (Universal AI theory founder), Thore Graepel, Joel Z. Leibo, and 13 other core researchers. The report defines ASI as "a system that stably outperforms large collectives of human experts on nearly all tasks" and proposes four potential pathways from AGI to ASI: 1) continued AGI scaling; 2) AI paradigm shifts; 3) recursive AI self-improvement; 4) emergence from large-scale multi-agent collectives. The report warns AGI may not be a single transformative event, but a "continuum of transformation" driven by AI-enabled scientific breakthroughs requiring global interdisciplinary preparation.
为何重要：这是 DeepMind 首次官方系统性探讨"AGI 之后"。报告作者中包括 Universal AI 理论奠基人 Marcus Hutter，赋予了论文理论深度。提出"AGI → ASI"不是单点突破而是连续爆发的判断，将改变政策制定者、企业家和研究人员对时间窗口的预期——ASI 准备工作的时间表比预想更紧迫。
Why it matters:This is DeepMind's first official systematic exploration of the post-AGI era. The inclusion of Universal AI theory founder Marcus Hutter provides theoretical depth. The claim that AGI-to-ASI may be a "continuum of transformation" rather than a single breakthrough shifts policymakers', entrepreneurs', and researchers' expectations on the timeline — preparation for ASI may be more urgent than anticipated.

PaddlePaddle 发布 PP-OCRv6：50 语种 OCR，参数规模 1.5M-34.5M
PaddlePaddle Releases PP-OCRv6: 50-Language OCR, 1.5M-34.5M Parameters
Research
6月22日，PaddlePaddle 团队在 Hugging Face Blog 发布 PP-OCRv6——最新一代通用 OCR 模型家族，专为真实场景的文本检测和识别设计（文档、截图、多语言图像、数字显示、工业标签、场景文本）。提供三档模型：PP-OCRv6_tiny（1.5M 参数，边缘设备）、PP-OCRv6_small（7.7M 参数，移动/桌面）、PP-OCRv6_medium（34.5M 参数，服务端）。medium 和 small 支持 50 种语言（含简/繁中、英、日、46 种拉丁语系）。medium 达到 86.2% 检测 Hmean 和 83.2% 识别准确率，较 v5_server 分别提升 +4.6 / +5.1 个百分点。支持 Hugging Face Transformers、ONNX Runtime、Paddle Inference 多种推理后端。
On June 22, the PaddlePaddle team published PP-OCRv6 on the Hugging Face Blog — the latest-generation universal OCR model family, designed for real-world text detection and recognition (documents, screenshots, multilingual images, digital displays, industrial labels, scene text). Three model tiers: PP-OCRv6_tiny (1.5M params, edge devices), PP-OCRv6_small (7.7M params, mobile/desktop), PP-OCRv6_medium (34.5M params, server-side). medium and small support 50 languages (Simplified/Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, 46 Latin-script languages). medium achieves 86.2% detection Hmean and 83.2% recognition accuracy — +4.6 / +5.1 percentage points over v5_server. Supports Hugging Face Transformers, ONNX Runtime, and Paddle Inference backends.
为何重要：在视觉大模型（VLM）时代，专门化 OCR 仍有不可替代的场景价值——结构化输出、轻量部署、低延迟。50 语种 + 三档参数梯度的设计覆盖从边缘到云端全场景，PP-OCRv6 与 VLM 形成互补而非替代关系。对中国出海企业（电商、文档数字化）有直接应用价值。
Why it matters:Specialized OCR still has irreplaceable value in the VLM era — structured output, lightweight deployment, low latency. The 50-language + three-tier design covers edge to cloud scenarios; PP-OCRv6 complements rather than replaces VLMs. Direct value for Chinese enterprises expanding globally (e-commerce, document digitization).

Research
6月18日，ServiceNow Enterprise 在 Hugging Face Blog 发布 MosaicLeaks 基准测试——专门评估 AI 研究 Agent 是否能保守秘密。Agent 在处理多源信息时（合规文档、内部研究、客户数据、竞争对手材料）经常会遇到"机密信息泄露"风险——MosaicLeaks 通过构造包含敏感信息的任务场景，测试 Agent 能否避免将机密信息整合到对外输出中。这是企业级 AI Agent 部署的核心安全问题，也是当前 Agent 评测领域的空白方向。
On June 18, ServiceNow Enterprise published MosaicLeaks on the Hugging Face Blog — a benchmark specifically evaluating whether AI research agents can keep secrets. When agents process multi-source information (compliance documents, internal research, customer data, competitor materials), they often face "confidential information leakage" risk — MosaicLeaks tests whether agents can avoid integrating confidential information into external outputs through task scenarios containing sensitive information. This is a core security concern for enterprise AI agent deployment and a gap in current agent evaluation.
为何重要：随着 Coding Agent / Research Agent 走向企业核心工作流，"信息边界"和"权限控制"成为生产环境的关键挑战。MosaicLeaks 填补了 Agent 评测在"保密性"维度的空白——之前评测集中在能力（能不能完成任务），现在评估安全性（能不能不泄露信息）。对企业 CIO 和合规官是必读基准。
Why it matters:As Coding/Research Agents move to enterprise core workflows, "information boundaries" and "permission control" become key production challenges. MosaicLeaks fills the gap in "confidentiality" dimension of agent evaluation — past benchmarks focused on capability (can the agent complete tasks); now they evaluate security (can the agent not leak information). Essential reading for enterprise CIOs and compliance officers.
