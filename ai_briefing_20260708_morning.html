Why it matters: GPT-5.6 shifts OpenAI’s naming from monolithic numbers to generation + durable capability tiers (Sol/Terra/Luna), signaling a productized release cadence; the government-preview process may also become a template for frontier model releases.

ByteDance Seed 发布 EdgeBench：真实世界环境学习扩展规律
ByteDance Seed releases EdgeBench, unveiling scaling laws of environment learning
🇨🇳 CN Research
7 月 2 日，ByteDance Seed 团队发布 EdgeBench，一个包含 134 个真实世界任务的超长程基准，用于衡量 AI 代理在环境中的学习能力而非单次表现。任务运行时长 12–72 小时，覆盖科学、软件工程、优化、知识工作、形式数学和游戏。研究发现，代理学习曲线可精确拟合为 log-sigmoid 函数，且环境学习速度大约每 3 个月翻一倍。团队已开源 51 个任务和完整评估框架。
On July 2, ByteDance Seed released EdgeBench, an ultra-long-horizon benchmark of 134 real-world tasks that measures how agents learn from environments rather than one-shot performance. Tasks run 12–72 hours across science, software engineering, optimization, knowledge work, formal math, and games. The study finds learning curves closely fit a log-sigmoid function and that environment-learning speed roughly doubles every three months. The team open-sourced 51 tasks and the full evaluation framework.
为何重要：当现有基准逐渐饱和，环境学习有望成为评估代理持续改进能力的下一前沿；EdgeBench 的 log-sigmoid 规律和每季度翻倍的速度预测，为评估代理能否“边做边学”提供了量化框架。
Why it matters: As static benchmarks saturate, environment learning may become the next frontier for measuring agents' ability to improve continuously; EdgeBench’s log-sigmoid law and its forecast of doubling learning speed every quarter provide a quantitative framework for evaluating "learning while doing."

为何重要：GeneBench-Pro 把评估重点从“知识 recall”转向“研究品味”——包括问题选择、假设修正、迭代实验和判断结果是否可决策，这正是 AI 在科学领域产生实际价值的关键差距。
