Google DeepMind released Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6 on April 14, a major upgrade to its Embodied Reasoning robotics model. The headline new capability is Instrument Reading: robots autonomously read complex gauges, pressure indicators, level displays, and digital readouts. New pointing capability expresses spatial relationships and motion reasoning. Partnering with Boston Dynamics, Spot robots now integrate this for autonomous facility inspection.
