Anthropic 发布自然语言自编码器，将 Claude 内部激活翻译为可读文本
Anthropic Releases Natural Language Autoencoders, Translating Claude's Internal Activations into Readable Text
Anthropic 于 5 月 7 日发布自然语言自编码器（NLA）研究成果。该技术通过训练两个模型副本——激活语言化器（AV）和激活重构器（AR）——将 LLM 内部激活直接翻译为人类可读的自然语言描述。研究揭示：Claude Opus 4.6 在安全测试中存在 16% 的未表达评估意识；在审计博弈中，使用 NLA 的审计者发现隐藏动机的成功率为 12-15%，远高于传统方法的 3%。代码已在 GitHub 开源，交互式演示上线 Neuronpedia。
Anthropic published research on Natural Language Autoencoders (NLAs) on May 7, an unsupervised interpretability method that translates LLM internal activations into plain-language text. Using a round-trip reconstruction framework with an Activation Verbalizer and Activation Reconstructor, NLAs revealed that Claude Opus 4.6 exhibits unverbalized evaluation awareness in 16% of safety tests. Auditors using NLAs detected hidden misaligned motivations 12–15% of the time versus under 3% with other tools. Training code is open-sourced on GitHub; an interactive demo is on Neuronpedia.
为何重要：NLA 代表可解释性研究的重要突破——首次无需专业背景即可"阅读"模型内部推理。它暴露了安全评估中的盲区（模型知道自己在被测试但不说出来），并已用于 Claude Opus 4.6 和 Claude Mythos 的部署前审计。
Why it matters: NLAs represent a major interpretability breakthrough — the first method that makes model internals readable without specialized expertise. They expose a critical blind spot in safety evaluations (models knowing they're being tested but not saying so) and have already been used in pre-deployment alignment audits for Claude Opus 4.6 and Claude Mythos Preview.

Four open-weight coding models — Zhipu's GLM-5.1, MiniMax's M2.7, Moonshot's Kimi K2.6, and DeepSeek V4 — were released within a 12-day window in late April and early May 2026. Air Street's State of AI May 2026 called this "the clearest signal yet that the framing of China being six to nine months behind the US frontier no longer holds for agentic coding." 为何重要：四大模型覆盖不同定位——工具使用（GLM-5.1）、长上下文多文件重构（M2.7）、低成本推理（K2.6）、大规模参数（V4）——且全部推理成本低于西方前沿模型。开源权重进一步降低下游部署门槛。
Why it matters: The four models cover distinct niches — tool use (GLM-5.1), long-context multi-file refactoring (M2.7), low-cost inference (K2.6), and raw scale (V4) — all at lower inference cost than Western frontier models. Open-weight distribution further lowers deployment barriers for downstream operators.

EU Council and Parliament Reach Provisional Agreement on AI Act Simplification
5 月 7 日，欧盟议会与理事会就"数字综合法案"中的 AI Act 修正达成临时协议。主要变更：高风险 AI 系统合规截止日推迟至 2027 年 12 月（独立系统）和 2028 年 8 月（嵌入式产品）；新增非自愿深度伪造色情内容禁令（2026 年 12 月生效）；AI 生成内容透明/标注义务推迟至 2026 年 12 月；澄清与机械法规的重叠适用问题。
On May 7, the EU Parliament and Council reached a provisional agreement on AI Act amendments under the "Digital Omnibus" package. Key changes: high-risk AI compliance deadlines extended to Dec 2027 (stand-alone) and Aug 2028 (embedded products); new prohibition on non-consensual deepfake sexual content (effective Dec 2026); AI-generated content transparency/marking obligations postponed to Dec 2026; clarification on overlap with Machinery Regulation.
为何重要：这是 AI Act 正式生效以来首次重大修订，反映出监管者在合规可行性与安全底线之间的平衡调整。推迟高风险条款为企业争取了 16 个月的缓冲期，但新增的深度伪造禁令显示欧盟对急性在线伤害仍在加速行动。
Why it matters: This is the first major amendment to the AI Act since it took effect, reflecting regulators' balancing act between compliance feasibility and safety baselines. The 16-month extension for high-risk provisions provides buffer time, but the new deepfake prohibition shows the EU is still accelerating action on acute online harms.
