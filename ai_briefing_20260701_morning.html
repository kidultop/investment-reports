OpenAI's First Custom Inference Chip Jalapeño: From Zero to Tape-out in 9 Months with Broadcom
US
6 月 24 日，OpenAI 与博通联合发布首款定制推理芯片 Jalapeño。采用 TSMC 3nm 制程、systolic array 架构搭配 HBM 高带宽内存，专为 LLM 推理场景量身打造，9 个月完成架构设计到流片。声称推理成本可降约 50%，预计 2026 年底进入数据中心部署。这是 OpenAI 从纯软件公司向硬件垂直整合迈出的第一步。
On June 24, OpenAI and Broadcom unveiled Jalapeño, a custom LLM inference ASIC. Built on TSMC 3nm with systolic array architecture and HBM memory, it went from architecture design to tape-out in just 9 months. Claims ~50% inference cost reduction, with data center deployment expected by late 2026. This marks OpenAI's first step from pure software to hardware vertical integration.
为何重要：OpenAI 正式进入芯片自研领域，与 Google（Axion）、Amazon（Trainium）、Microsoft（Maia 200）形成 AI 巨头自研芯片四方格局，对 NVIDIA GPU 依赖正在系统性降低。
Why it matters:OpenAI joins Google (Axion), Amazon (Trainium), and Microsoft (Maia 200) in custom AI silicon, creating a four-way格局 of hyperscaler chip self-development systematically reducing NVIDIA GPU dependency.

Key highlight: trained entirely on 50,000+ domestic compute chips, consuming 35T tokens with no rollback or loss spikes. 为何重要：这是首个在纯国产算力集群上完成万亿参数级前沿模型训练的开源项目，验证了国产算力平台的大规模训练可行性。LSA 稀疏注意力是对 DeepSeek DSA 的工程优化迭代，对长上下文推理效率有重要影响。
Why it matters:First open-source trillion-parameter frontier model trained entirely on domestic (Chinese) compute chips, validating the feasibility of large-scale training on non-NVIDIA hardware. LSA sparse attention is an engineering optimization over DeepSeek's DSA, with significant implications for long-context inference efficiency.

Colorado AI 法正式生效：美国首个州级 AI 监管法律从理论走向执行
Colorado AI Act Takes Effect: First US State AI Law Moves from Theory to Enforcement
Policy US
6 月 30 日，Colorado 的《消费者 AI 保护法案》（SB24-205）正式生效，这是美国首个广泛覆盖的州级 AI 法律。对招聘、贷款、住房、医疗、教育、法律服务等"高风险 AI 系统"的开发者和部署者施加严格的透明度与风险管理义务。与此同时，联邦层面的《伟大美国 AI 法案》（Great American AI Act）269 页草案正在推进，拟 3 年内 preempt 所有州级 AI 法律。
On June 30, Colorado's Consumer Protections for AI Act (SB24-205) took effect — the first broad US state AI law. It imposes strict transparency and risk-management obligations on developers and deployers of "high-risk AI systems" in hiring, lending, housing, healthcare, education, and legal services. Meanwhile, the 269-page federal Great American AI Act draft is advancing, proposing to preempt all state AI laws for 3 years.
为何重要：美国 AI 监管从抽象原则进入可执行规则阶段。州-联邦之间的管辖权争夺将直接影响 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google 等前沿模型厂商的合规策略。
Why it matters:US AI regulation moves from abstract principles to enforceable rules. The state-federal jurisdiction battle will directly impact compliance strategies for OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and other frontier model developers.
