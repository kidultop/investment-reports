OpenAI Forms $4B Deployment Company Subsidiary, Acquires Tomoro for Enterprise AI

The company simultaneously acquired UK-based AI consultancy Tomoro, bringing 150 specialized AI engineers to accelerate enterprise LLM adoption across finance, healthcare, and manufacturing sectors.

Why it matters: This marks OpenAI's direct competition with Anthropic in enterprise AI services. Anthropic launched its own financial-sector AI agent suite in early May; OpenAI's higher-capital response signals a duopoly forming in the enterprise AI market.

Google DeepMind 发布 Magic Pointer，用 AI 重新定义鼠标指针交互范式
DeepMind Unveils Magic Pointer: AI That Understands "This" and "That" Gestures
US
5 月 12 日，Google DeepMind 在 AndroidShow | I/O Edition 活动上发布 Magic Pointer 博文，详细介绍了重新设计 AI 时代鼠标指针的核心理念。系统融合四大交互原则：维持操作流（在所有应用中无缝工作）、展示即告知（自动捕获屏幕视觉语义）、"这/那"自然手势理解、以及像素到可操作实体的转换。目前 Chrome 已上线指针唤起 Gemini 功能，Googlebook（新款笔记本电脑系列）即将推送 Magic Pointer。
On May 12, Google DeepMind published "Reimagining the Mouse Pointer for the AI Era," outlining four core interaction principles: maintaining workflow, auto-capturing visual-semantic context, understanding "this/that" gestures, and converting pixels to actionable entities. Chrome already has Gemini-powered pointer features live; Magic Pointer is rolling out to Googlebook laptops soon.
为何重要：50 年来鼠标指针的功能几乎未变，该项目将 AI 能力嵌入操作系统底层交互层，用自然手势替代复杂的文本提示，标志着 AI 从独立工具向操作系统原生组件的深度融合。
Why it matters: The mouse pointer has seen no functional evolution in 50+ years. Embedding AI into the OS's fundamental interaction layer — replacing complex text prompts with natural gestures — signals AI's shift from standalone tools to native OS components.
🔗 来源：Google DeepMind Blog

Why it matters: AISI's cyber range evaluation is becoming a key credential for AI security; Claude Mythos's full pass gives Anthropic a differentiated trust advantage, with significant implications for government procurement and enterprise compliance.
