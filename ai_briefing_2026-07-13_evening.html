OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.6 系列模型
OpenAI releases GPT-5.6 family: Sol, Terra, and Luna
OpenAI 于 7 月 9 日发布 GPT-5.6 系列，包含旗舰 Sol、均衡 Terra 与低成本 Luna 三个层级。Sol 在 AA Coding Agent Index、BrowseComp、OSWorld 2.0 与网络安全基准上取得领先，并支持 Ultra 模式默认协调 4 个智能体并行处理复杂任务；Terra 与 Luna 则以约 Fable 5 成本的 1/16 提供可竞争力。系列引入程序化工具调用（内存内工具协调）与零数据保留兼容，定价为 Sol $5/$30、Terra $2.50/$15、Luna $1/$6（每百万 token）。
On July 9, OpenAI launched the GPT-5.6 family with three tiers: flagship Sol, balanced Terra, and cost-efficient Luna. Sol leads on coding (AA Coding Agent Index: 80), browsing (BrowseComp: 92.2%), computer use (OSWorld 2.0: 62.6%), and cyber benchmarks, and defaults to coordinating four parallel agents in Ultra mode. Terra and Luna aim for price-performance parity at roughly 1/16 the cost of Fable 5. The family introduces programmatic in-memory tool calling and Zero Data Retention compatibility.
为何重要：这是 OpenAI 首次在单一品牌下同时提供旗舰、均衡与低成本三档模型，并以 Ultra 模式将多智能体协作设为默认，标志着从对话模型向任务执行平台的进一步迁移。
Why it matters: This is OpenAI's first simultaneous release of flagship, balanced, and cost-optimized tiers under one family, with Ultra mode making multi-agent collaboration the default — signaling a further shift from chatbot to task-execution platform.

Anthropic 发现 Claude 内部存在全局工作空间 J-space
Anthropic identifies a global workspace inside Claude: J-space
Anthropic 于 7 月 6 日发布研究《A Global Workspace in Language Models》，利用 Jacobian Lens（J-lens）技术在 Claude 内部发现一个自发涌现的特权神经工作空间 J-space。该空间包含少量与词汇关联的激活模式，具备可报告、可按指令控制、因果参与推理、跨任务广播四大特征。删除 J-space 会摧毁多步推理与摘要能力，但保留语法和简单事实检索。研究同时开源了实现与 Neuronpedia 交互演示。
On July 6, Anthropic published 'A Global Workspace in Language Models,' using its Jacobian Lens (J-lens) technique to discover a spontaneously emergent privileged workspace inside Claude called J-space. It contains a small set of word-linked neural activation patterns that are reportable, voluntarily controllable, causally involved in reasoning, and flexibly broadcast across tasks. Ablating J-space destroys multi-step reasoning and summarization while leaving grammar and simple fact retrieval intact. Code and a Neuronpedia demo were released.
为何重要：J-space 为 AI 安全提供了可观察内部意图的新窗口，也暗示类似人脑全局工作空间的结构可能是智能系统解决特定问题的通用计算方案，而非生物大脑独有。
Why it matters: J-space offers a new window into monitoring hidden model intentions for safety, and suggests that workspace-like structures may be a general computational solution for intelligence, not unique to biological brains.

Google DeepMind 开源 Gemma 4 模型系列
Google DeepMind releases open Gemma 4 model family
Google DeepMind 发布 Gemma 4 开源模型，提供 E2B/E4B（边缘设备）、12B、26B 与 31B 参数规模。31B IT Thinking 在 MMMU Pro、AIME 2026、LiveCodeBench v6、GPQA Diamond 与 τ²-bench 等多项基准上显著超越前代 Gemma 3 27B，并在部分指标接近闭源前沿模型。新模型支持原生多模态、140 种语言与 Function Calling，全系采用 Apache 2.0 许可。
Google DeepMind released the open Gemma 4 family in sizes E2B/E4B for edge devices, 12B, 26B, and 31B. The 31B IT Thinking variant substantially outperforms Gemma 3 27B on MMMU Pro, AIME 2026, LiveCodeBench v6, GPQA Diamond, and τ²-bench, and approaches closed-frontier levels on several tasks. The models support native multimodality, 140 languages, and function calling, and are licensed under Apache 2.0.
为何重要：Gemma 4 将接近前沿的多模态与智能体能力以完全开源、可商用许可推向个人开发者与边缘设备，可能加速端侧 AI 应用与本地优先部署的普及。
Why it matters: Gemma 4 brings near-frontier multimodal and agentic capabilities to individual developers and edge devices under a fully open, commercially permissive license, potentially accelerating on-device and local-first AI adoption.

欧盟 AI 法案 GPAI 罚款条款将于 8 月 2 日生效
EU AI Act GPAI fine provisions take effect on August 2
根据欧盟《人工智能法案》第 101 条，针对通用人工智能模型（GPAI）提供商的罚款权力将于 2026 年 8 月 2 日正式生效。违规最高可处全球年营业额 3% 或 1500 万欧元（以较高者为准）。该条款覆盖文档、信息请求、评估配合等义务。
Under Article 101 of the EU AI Act, the European Commission's power to fine general-purpose AI model providers becomes enforceable on August 2, 2026. Penalties can reach up to 3% of global annual turnover or €15 million, whichever is higher, covering documentation, information requests, and evaluation cooperation obligations.
为何重要：罚款权生效将 GPAI 合规从纸面义务转化为实际风险，迫使模型提供商在 8 月前完善技术文档、版权政策与监管响应流程。
Why it matters: The fine authority turns GPAI compliance from a paper obligation into real risk, forcing model providers to complete technical documentation, copyright policies, and regulatory response procedures before August.
