4 月 2 日，Google DeepMind 正式发布 Gemma 4 系列，包含 E2B、E4B、26B MoE、31B Dense 四款规格，覆盖树莓派到单卡 H100 的完整硬件区间。31B Dense 模型在 Arena AI 开源排行榜冲至全球第三；26B MoE 模型仅激活 38 亿参数即可达到同级 Dense 模型效果。全系列采用 Apache 2.0 许可，完全开源、可商用。基于 Gemini 3 同款技术栈构建，支持多模态、超长上下文。
On April 2, Google DeepMind released Gemma 4 with four model sizes: E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense, covering hardware from Raspberry Pi to a single H100. The 31B Dense model ranks 3rd on Arena AI's open-source leaderboard. The 26B MoE activates only 3.8B parameters to match same-tier dense models. All models use Apache 2.0 licensing, fully open-source and commercially usable, built on the same technology stack as Gemini 3 with multimodal and extended context support.
为何重要：Gemma 4 以极高的参数效率（38 亿激活参数打 Dense 同级）和完全开放的 Apache 2.0 许可，直接挑战 Meta Llama 在开源生态的统治地位，将进一步降低企业部署多模态 AI 的门槛。
Why it matters: Gemma 4's exceptional parameter efficiency (3.8B active parameters matching same-tier dense models) and full Apache 2.0 licensing directly challenge Meta Llama's dominance in the open-source ecosystem, further lowering barriers for enterprise multimodal AI deployment.
