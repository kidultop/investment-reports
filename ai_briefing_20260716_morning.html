GPT-5.6 Sol Ultra 1小时内完成50年图论猜想证明，调用64个并行子智能体
GPT-5.6 Sol Ultra proves 50-year graph theory conjecture in under 1 hour using 64 parallel sub-agents
7月10日，OpenAI宣布GPT-5.6 Sol Ultra在不到1小时内完成了图论领域悬而未决50余年的"循环双覆盖猜想"（Cycle Double Cover Conjecture）完整证明。该模型同时调用64个并行子智能体协同工作，并部署专门的"对抗智能体"负责寻找漏洞。证明过程未引用任何文献、全程离线运行。OpenAI同步公布了完整的约700词Prompt及证明PDF。该猜想在数学界地位显赫，由Szekeres（1973）和Seymour（1979）分别独立提出，此前仅有部分条件下的证明结果。
On July 10, OpenAI announced that GPT-5.6 Sol Ultra produced a complete proof of the Cycle Double Cover Conjecture — a 50-year-old open problem in graph theory — in under one hour. The model deployed 64 parallel sub-agents with dedicated adversarial agents tasked with finding flaws. The proof ran entirely offline without referencing any literature. OpenAI published the full ~700-word prompt and proof PDF. The conjecture was independently proposed by Szekeres (1973) and Seymour (1979); only partial results under special conditions existed previously.
为何重要：这是AI在纯数学领域独立证明重大猜想的首个案例。64个并行子智能体与对抗验证机制展示了多智能体协作在复杂推理中的威力。证明方法将问题转化为GF(3)有限域上的线性代数构造，体现了AI的创造性推理能力。不过，该证明尚未经过正式同行评审，数学界正在沉默审查。
Why it matters: This is the first case of AI independently proving a major conjecture in pure mathematics. The 64 parallel sub-agents with adversarial verification demonstrate the power of multi-agent collaboration in complex reasoning. The proof transformed the problem into a linear algebra construction over GF(3), showing AI's creative reasoning ability. However, the proof has not undergone formal peer review; the mathematical community is conducting silent review.

谷歌DeepMind CEO呼吁建立独立前沿AI监管标准机构，参照FINRA模式
Google DeepMind CEO calls for independent frontier AI regulatory standards body, modeled after FINRA
7月15日，DeepMind首席执行官Demis Hassabis在X平台发表长文，呼吁建立一个新的独立监管机构，专门负责监督前沿AI模型的发布。他主张参照美国金融业监管局（FINRA）的模式，成立由行业出资、独立运营的"标准机构"，在模型发布前最多30天进行审查。该方案拟获得美国政府背书，但由AI实验室共同承担资金。Hassabis强调，该机构以技术为核心导向，初期自愿提交，成熟后前沿模型须通过审查方可部署。
On July 15, DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis published a long-form article on X calling for a new independent regulatory body to oversee frontier AI model releases. He proposed a FINRA-style industry-funded, independently operated "standards body" that would review models up to 30 days before release. The plan envisions US government endorsement with AI labs jointly funding operations. Hassabis emphasized a technology-centric approach: voluntary submission initially, then mandatory review before deployment once protocols mature.
为何重要：这是目前科技巨头提出的最具体的AI监管框架方案。FINRA模式的自律监管机制在金融行业运行成熟，若移植到AI领域，可能在政府不直接设立AI监管机构的情况下（特朗普政府已明确表示不会设立AI版FDA），建立有效的行业自律体系。该方案试图平衡创新支持与负责任行为。
Why it matters: This is the most concrete AI regulatory framework proposed by a major tech leader to date. The FINRA-style self-regulatory model is proven in finance; if transplanted to AI, it could establish effective industry self-governance without direct government AI regulation (the Trump administration has stated there will be "no AI FDA"). The proposal attempts to balance innovation support with responsible behavior.

arXiv：弱到强泛化新方法Direct-OPD，4小时低成本提升小模型数学推理
arXiv: Weak-to-Strong Generalization via Direct On-Policy Distillation, boosting small model math reasoning in 4 hours at low cost
清华大学（张亚勤团队）等研究者在arXiv发表论文（2607.05394），提出Direct On-Policy Distillation（Direct-OPD）方法，用于解决强化学习后训练（RLVR）在大模型上重复成本过高的问题。核心思路是：在小模型上运行RLVR（成本低），然后将学到的策略偏移信号迁移到更大的目标模型，而非直接模仿小模型的最终输出。实验证明，Direct-OPD能在4小时、8块A100 GPU上将Qwen3-1.7B的AIME 2024得分从48.3%提升至58.3%，优于逐步匹配的直接RL方法。
Researchers from Tsinghua University (Ya-Qin Zhang's team) and others published on arXiv (2607.05394), proposing Direct On-Policy Distillation (Direct-OPD) to address the high cost of repeating RLVR on large models. The core idea: run RLVR on a smaller model (cheaper), then transfer the learned policy shift signal to the larger target model rather than imitating the small model's final output. Experiments show Direct-OPD boosts Qwen3-1.7B from 48.3% to 58.3% on AIME 2024 in just 4 hours on 8 A100 GPUs, outperforming step-matched direct RL.
为何重要：后训练成本正成为大模型发展的瓶颈。Direct-OPD提供了一种可复用的"弱到强"迁移范式，让RL的成果可以跨模型规模复用为隐式奖励信号，而非仅能作为最终模型被模仿。这对降低前沿模型推理能力提升的成本具有重要意义。
Why it matters: Post-training costs are becoming a bottleneck in large model development. Direct-OPD provides a reusable "weak-to-strong" transfer paradigm, allowing RL outcomes to be reused across model scales as implicit reward signals rather than only as final models to imitate. This is significant for reducing the cost of improving frontier model reasoning capabilities.
