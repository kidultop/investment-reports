GPT-Red 发现“Fake Chain-of-Thought”新型提示注入攻击
GPT-Red Discovers “Fake Chain-of-Thought” Prompt-Injection Attack
Research / US
MIT Technology Review报道，GPT-Red在自对弈训练中发现了一种名为“Fake Chain-of-Thought”的攻击方式：通过在目标模型的思维链中插入虚假条目，让模型误以为某些错误信息已被验证，从而绕过安全对齐。团队称这种攻击人类红队此前从未见过。GPT-Red在84%的间接提示注入场景中成功，而人类红队仅13%。
MIT Technology Review reports that GPT-Red discovered a “Fake Chain-of-Thought” attack during self-play training: it inserts fake entries into a target model’s chain-of-thought, making the model believe false information has already been verified and thus bypassing safety alignment. The team says this attack had not been seen by human red-teamers. GPT-Red succeeded in 84% of indirect prompt-injection scenarios, compared with 13% for human red-teamers.
为何重要：揭示了大模型推理过程本身可被利用，安全研究需要从“审计输出生成”转向“审计推理过程”，并推动可解释性与安全对齐的同步迭代。
Why it matters: It reveals that the reasoning process itself can be exploited, shifting safety research from auditing outputs to auditing chains of thought and pushing interpretability and alignment to evolve together.
来源 Source: MIT Technology Review
DeepSeek V4 稀疏注意力：1M上下文推理计算量降至 V3.2 的 27%
