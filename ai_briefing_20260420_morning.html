新加坡政府宣布 10 亿新元国家 AI 研究投资计划
Singapore Government Announces S$1 Billion National AI Research Investment Plan
🇸🇬 SG
新加坡数字发展与信息部（MDDI）宣布将在 2025-2030 年间投资超过 10 亿新元，用于国家人工智能研究与发展计划（NAIRD）。该计划属于国家人工智能战略 2.0（NAIS 2.0）的组成部分，重点聚焦三大领域：基础 AI 研究（建立负责任 AI、资源高效 AI 等卓越研究中心）、应用 AI 研究（支持产业界 AI 落地）、以及从大学前教育到高校教职的完整 AI 人才培养体系。
Singapore's Ministry of Digital Development and Information (MDDI) announced an investment of over S$1 billion from 2025-2030 for the National AI Research and Development (NAIRD) Plan. Part of the National AI Strategy 2.0 (NAIS 2.0), it focuses on three pillars: foundational AI research (establishing Research Centres of Excellence for Responsible AI and Resource-efficient AI), applied AI research for industry adoption, and comprehensive AI talent development from pre-university to faculty levels.
为何重要：这是新加坡在 AI 领域最大规模的政府投资之一，标志着新加坡从"AI 应用者"向"AI 原创研究贡献者"的战略转型，将直接提升 NUS、NTU 等高校的国际竞争力。
Why it matters: This represents one of Singapore's largest government investments in AI, signaling a strategic shift from "AI adopter" to "AI research contributor" and directly enhancing the international competitiveness of NUS, NTU, and other institutions.

阿里通义千问开源 Qwen3.6-35B-A3B 模型
Alibaba Qwen Open-Sources Qwen3.6-35B-A3B Model
🇨🇳 CN
阿里云通义千问团队开源发布 Qwen3.6-35B-A3B 模型，采用稀疏激活架构（总参数 350 亿，激活参数仅 30 亿）。该模型针对代码智能体场景深度优化，支持思维保持功能以解决长上下文丢失问题。作为 Qwen3.6 系列的首个开源版本，它在保持高效推理的同时，实现了接近更大规模模型的代码理解与生成能力。
Alibaba Cloud's Qwen team open-sourced the Qwen3.6-35B-A3B model featuring sparse activation architecture (35B total parameters, only 3B activated per token). Deeply optimized for code agent scenarios, it supports thought persistence to solve long-context loss issues. As the first open-source release in the Qwen3.6 series, it achieves code understanding and generation capabilities comparable to much larger models while maintaining efficient inference.
为何重要：稀疏架构（MoE）正成为高效大模型的主流方向，Qwen3.6-35B-A3B 以较小激活参数实现强大代码能力，为开发者提供了高性价比的本地部署选择，同时推动开源社区对高效架构的探索。
Why it matters: Sparse architecture (MoE) is becoming mainstream for efficient LLMs. Qwen3.6-35B-A3B delivers strong coding capabilities with minimal activated parameters, offering developers a cost-effective local deployment option while advancing the open-source community's exploration of efficient architectures.
🔗 来源 Source: Qwen.ai
