OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.6 系列：Sol/Terra/Luna 三型号齐发，美国政府首次介入发布节奏
OpenAI Unveils GPT-5.6 Family: Sol, Terra, Luna Launch as Limited Preview Under US Government Directive
US
北京时间 6 月 27 日，OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.6 系列，包含旗舰 Sol（Terminal-Bench 2.1 领先 Claude Mythos 5 达 4 个百分点）、中量级 Terra（性能持平 GPT-5.5 但成本减半）和轻量级 Luna。应美国政府要求，当前仅以「受信合作伙伴限量预览」方式上线，全面开放时间未定。Sol 运行于 Cerebras 晶圆级推理芯片，最高可达 750 tokens/秒。同时，系统卡披露三款型号均在网络安全与生物/化学领域被标记为「高风险」——这是 OpenAI 历史上首次全系触及高级警戒线。
On June 27, OpenAI launched the GPT-5.6 family: flagship Sol (4 pts ahead of Claude Mythos 5 on Terminal-Bench 2.1), mid-tier Terra (GPT-5.5-level at half cost), and lightweight Luna. Per US government request, it ships as "trusted partner limited preview" — full access timeline unconfirmed. Sol runs on Cerebras wafer-scale chips at up to 750 tokens/sec. Notably, the system card reveals all three models scored "High Risk" in cybersecurity and bio/chem domains — a first for OpenAI where every variant crosses the elevated threshold.
为何重要：这标志着美国政府首次公开介入 OpenAI 旗舰模型发布节奏。从 Anthropic Fable 5 的出口管制到 GPT-5.6 的限量预览，前沿 AI 模型的部署正从企业自主决策转向「政府-企业协同审查」模式。GPT-5.6 全系高风险评估也表明，能力提升正伴随安全风险的系统性蔓延。
Why it matters: This marks the first time the US government has openly intervened in OpenAI's flagship model release cadence. From the Fable 5 export controls to GPT-5.6's limited preview, frontier AI deployment is shifting from company autonomy to a government-enterprise co-review model. The family-wide High Risk designation also signals that capability gains are spreading safety risks across all model tiers, not just flagships.

OpenAI 发布首款自研 AI 芯片 Jalapeño：9 个月流片，推理成本降低 50%
OpenAI Debuts First Custom AI Chip "Jalapeño": 9-Month Tapeout, ~50% Inference Cost Reduction
US
美东时间 6 月 24 日，OpenAI 与博通联合发布首款自研 AI 推理芯片 Jalapeño。该芯片从设计到流片仅 9 个月，创高性能 ASIC 最快开发周期纪录。早期测试显示每瓦性能「显著优于当前最先进 AI 加速器」，推理成本约降低 50%。博通 CEO 陈福阳透露，Jalapeño 将于今年开始部署于千兆瓦级数据中心，且这只是「多代路线图的起点」。OpenAI 借此正式从纯模型公司转型为全栈 AI 基础设施提供商。
On June 24, OpenAI and Broadcom jointly unveiled Jalapeño, OpenAI's first custom AI inference chip. The ASIC went from design to tapeout in just 9 months — a record for high-performance custom silicon. Early tests show per-watt performance "significantly better than current state-of-the-art AI accelerators," with ~50% inference cost savings. Broadcom CEO Hock Tan confirmed gigawatt-scale data center deployment starting this year, calling it "the start of a multi-generation roadmap." OpenAI now transitions from pure model company to full-stack AI infrastructure provider.
为何重要：Jalapeño 的发布标志着 AI 竞争从模型层扩展到芯片层，OpenAI 加入谷歌、微软、亚马逊的「去英伟达化」阵营。9 个月流片周期展示了 AI 辅助芯片设计的潜力，千兆瓦级部署野心表明 OpenAI 正为 AGI 级算力需求进行结构性布局。
Why it matters: Jalapeño extends AI competition from models to silicon, placing OpenAI alongside Google, Microsoft, and Amazon in the "de-NVIDIA-ification" race. The 9-month tapeout demonstrates AI-assisted chip design potential, and gigawatt-scale ambitions reveal structural preparation for AGI-level compute demands.

DeepSeek 开源 DSpark：V4 推理速度提升 85%，MIT 协议发布
DeepSeek Open-Sources DSpark: V4-Flash Inference Up to 85% Faster, MIT License
CN
6 月 27 日，DeepSeek 开源 DSpark 推测解码框架，以 MIT 协议发布。该框架通过半自回归生成、置信度调度和零开销调度三项技术创新，在不改变模型权重的前提下，使 V4-Flash 推理速度提升 60%-85%，V4-Pro 提升 57%-78%。伴随发布的 DeepSpec 工具包支持 Qwen3、Gemma 等第三方模型族，并提供完整的训练与评估管线。
On June 27, DeepSeek open-sourced DSpark, a speculative decoding framework under MIT license. Using three innovations — semi-autoregressive generation, confidence-scheduled verification, and zero-overhead scheduling — DSpark delivers 60-85% faster V4-Flash and 57-78% faster V4-Pro inference without modifying model weights. The companion DeepSpec toolkit supports Qwen3 and Gemma model families with full training/evaluation pipelines.
为何重要：DSpark 展示了推理优化正成为模型竞争的「第二战场」——不改变模型能力，仅通过工程优化即可将吞吐量翻倍。其 MIT 开源协议和跨模型兼容性意味着该技术可被全球开发者社区直接采用，进一步降低大模型部署成本。
Why it matters: DSpark demonstrates that inference optimization is becoming a "second battlefield" in model competition — doubling throughput through engineering alone without touching model weights. MIT licensing and cross-model compatibility mean the global developer community can adopt it immediately, further lowering LLM deployment costs.

DeepSeek DSpark 技术解析：半自回归生成 + 置信度调度 + 零开销调度
DSpark Technical Deep Dive: Semi-Autoregressive Generation + Confidence Scheduling + Zero-Overhead Scheduling
Research
DSpark 在标准推测解码基础上引入三项创新：(1) 半自回归生成——快速并行骨干 + 轻量马尔可夫头，解决草稿 token 后缀衰减问题，宏平均接受长度较 Eagle3 提升 30.9%；(2) 置信度调度验证——硬件感知前缀调度器根据 GPU 负载动态截断验证长度，消除无效验证计算；(3) 零开销调度——异步预测最优截断长度，CUDA 图连续重放无停顿。DeepSpec 工具包同步发布，原生支持 Qwen3 和 Gemma 模型族。
DSpark introduces three innovations atop standard speculative decoding: (1) Semi-autoregressive generation — fast parallel backbone + lightweight Markov head to combat draft suffix decay, improving macro-average acceptance length by 30.9% over Eagle3; (2) Confidence-scheduled verification — hardware-aware prefix scheduler dynamically truncates verification based on GPU load; (3) Zero-overhead scheduling — async prediction of optimal truncation, enabling uninterrupted CUDA graph replay. The DeepSpec toolkit ships with native Qwen3 and Gemma support.
为何重要：DSpark 的三项创新分别解决推测解码的三大痛点（草稿质量、验证浪费、调度延迟），为大规模生产部署提供了实用方案。MIT 开源 + 跨模型兼容意味着该技术栈可成为行业标准推理加速方案。
Why it matters: DSpark's three innovations address speculative decoding's three major pain points (draft quality, verification waste, scheduling latency), offering a practical solution for large-scale production deployment. MIT licensing + cross-model compatibility could make this the industry-standard inference acceleration stack.

EU AI Act Digital Omnibus 通过：高风险 AI 合规延期至 2027 年 12 月
EU AI Act Digital Omnibus Adopted: High-Risk AI Compliance Deferred to December 2027
Policy
6 月 16 日，欧洲议会以 423 票赞成通过 AI Act Digital Omnibus 修订案。核心变化：原定 2026 年 8 月 2 日的高风险 AI 系统（Annex III）合规义务延期至 2027 年 12 月 2 日；嵌入式高风险 AI（Annex I）延期至 2028 年 8 月。GPAI 模型义务、第 50 条透明度要求和处罚制度不受影响，仍于 2026 年 8 月 2 日生效。修订案还新增对 AI 生成非自愿亲密图像和 CSAM 的禁令，并为中小企业提供合规减免。法案需经欧盟理事会正式通过并在官方公报发布后生效。
On June 16, the European Parliament adopted the AI Act Digital Omnibus with 423 votes. Key changes: standalone high-risk AI (Annex III) compliance deferred from August 2, 2026 to December 2, 2027; embedded high-risk AI (Annex I) deferred to August 2028. GPAI model obligations, Article 50 transparency, and penalty regime remain unaffected — still effective August 2, 2026. The Omnibus also bans AI-generated non-consensual intimate imagery and CSAM, and provides SME compliance relief. Formal Council adoption and Official Journal publication are still pending for legal effect.
为何重要：欧盟给高风险 AI 合规提供了 16 个月的缓冲期，但 GPAI 模型的透明度和内容标注义务 8 月即生效。对于 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google 等 GPAI 提供商，这意味着必须在未来 33 天内完成技术文档和版权合规准备。延期也反映出欧盟在创新促进与风险管控之间的务实平衡。
Why it matters: The EU granted 16 months of relief for high-risk AI compliance, but GPAI transparency and content marking obligations take effect in August — giving OpenAI, Anthropic, Google and others just 33 days to complete technical documentation and copyright compliance. The deferral also reflects the EU's pragmatic balance between innovation promotion and risk control.

Singapore Launches Government-Wide AI Agent Registry for 150K Officers; PAIR Adoption Exceeds 50%
🇸🇬 SG
6 月 2 日，新加坡 GovTech 宣布正在构建面向全政府 15 万公务员的 AI Agent 注册表，嵌入 AI Assistant Desk 平台。注册表追踪每个 Agent 的所有者、授权行为和每日活动日志，并配备运行时护栏（禁止删文件、限制外部邮件、自动敏感词扫描）。PAIR Chat 目前已有超过 50% 公务员（约 7.5 万人）日常使用，公务员已自主构建约 1.8 万个定制 AI Bot。平台目前处于试点测试阶段，计划 2026 年下半年全面推广。此外，新加坡于 5 月 20 日更新了国家 AI 战略（NAIS），提出十大更新优先事项，聚焦制造业、金融、医疗和互联互通四大国家 AI 使命。
On June 2, Singapore's GovTech announced a government-wide AI agent registry for 150,000 public officers, embedded in the AI Assistant Desk platform. The registry tracks agent ownership, permitted actions, and daily activity logs, with runtime guardrails (block file deletion, restrict external email, auto-scan for offensive content). Over 50% of officers (~75K) now use PAIR Chat daily, with ~18,000 custom AI bots built by officers themselves. The platform is in pilot testing with wider rollout planned for H2 2026. Separately, Singapore updated its National AI Strategy (NAIS) on May 20 with 10 refreshed priorities and four national AI Missions in manufacturing, finance, healthcare, and connectivity.
为何重要：新加坡的 AI Agent 注册表可能是全球首个政府级 AI 代理治理系统——不是理论框架而是正在运行的平台。其设计哲学（运行时护栏而非事后审查、模型无关的安全层）为其他国家的公共部门 AI 部署提供了可复制的范式。50%+ 的 PAIR 使用率表明公务员 AI 采用已过临界点。
Why it matters: Singapore's AI Agent Registry may be the world's first operational government-level AI agent governance system — not a theoretical framework but a running platform. Its design philosophy (runtime guardrails over post-hoc review, model-agnostic security layer) offers a replicable paradigm for public sector AI deployment globally. The 50%+ PAIR adoption rate signals that civil service AI usage has passed the critical mass threshold.

DeepSeek DSpark + DeepSpec Dual Open-Source: MIT License, Qwen3/Gemma Support
Open

Pre-trained DSpark checkpoints work directly via vLLM or SGLang without running the full training pipeline.
为何重要：DeepSeek 正在构建一个「开源推理加速标准」——不是只给代码，而是给完整的训练到部署工具链。跨模型兼容性意味着 DSpark 可以成为社区通用的推理加速层，而非 DeepSeek 专属优化。
Why it matters: DeepSeek is building an "open-source inference acceleration standard" — not just code, but a complete training-to-deployment toolchain. Cross-model compatibility means DSpark can become a community-universal inference acceleration layer rather than a DeepSeek-specific optimization.
