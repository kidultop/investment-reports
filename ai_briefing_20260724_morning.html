OpenAI 发布 Presence：面向企业的可管控智能体运营平台
OpenAI Launches Presence: A Governable Agent Operations Platform for Enterprises
US
7 月 22 日，OpenAI 正式发布企业级 AI 智能体运营平台 Presence，将其战略从「卖模型」转向「卖企业软件服务」。Presence 将智能体与企业内部数据、管理制度、现有软件及业务流程深度链接，覆盖客服、销售、IT 服务等场景，支持语音与聊天双模态。平台提供模拟测试、安全护栏、人工复核与自动评分等治理工具；生产环境运行后，Codex 通过 Presence 插件分析质量信号并提出改进建议，团队可对照生产版本测试变更并批准受控上线。Presence 目前仅以有限通用可用性（GA）向符合条件的企业客户开放，必须由 OpenAI 前方部署工程师或授权合作伙伴完成部署，不能自助开通。OpenAI 称其英语电话客服通道（1-888-GPT-0090）已由 Presence 支撑，可无人协助解决 75% 呼入问题，Codex 驱动的改进循环在 10 天内将人工接管率降低 15 个百分点（公司自报，未经独立验证）。BBVA、软银、澳大利亚 IAG 等机构正在评估。
On July 22, OpenAI formally launched Presence, an enterprise AI-agent operations platform marking its strategic pivot from "selling models" to "selling enterprise software services." Presence deeply links agents to a company's internal data, policies, existing software, and business workflows across customer support, sales, and IT service, with both voice and chat modalities. It provides governance tooling — simulation testing, guardrails, human review, and automated grading — and after production deployment, Codex analyzes quality signals via the Presence plugin and proposes improvements, which teams test against the live version before approving a controlled rollout. Presence is available only through a limited GA program to eligible enterprise customers and must be deployed by OpenAI Forward Deployed Engineers or authorized partners — not self-serve. OpenAI says its English phone support line (1-888-GPT-0090) already runs on Presence, resolving 75% of inbound calls unattended, with the Codex-driven improvement loop cutting human-takeover rate by 15 points in 10 days (company-reported, unverified). BBVA, SoftBank, and Australia's IAG are evaluating.
为何重要：这是 OpenAI 成立以来最明确的一次战略转身——用高触达工程服务模式挑战 Microsoft、Salesforce 等企业软件巨头。部署模式类似 Palantir 的前方部署工程师，但更聚焦智能体治理。它与 Anthropic 一周前上线的 Ode 咨询组织形成直接对位，预示着前沿实验室的竞争从「模型能力」转向「交付与运维能力」。
Why it matters:This is OpenAI's clearest strategic turn yet — challenging enterprise-software incumbents like Microsoft and Salesforce via a high-touch deployment model. The approach resembles Palantir's forward-deployed-engineer model but focuses on agent governance. It directly counters Anthropic's Ode consulting org launched a week earlier, signaling the frontier-lab race is shifting from "model capability" to "delivery and operations capability."

AMD 与 Anthropic 达成 2GW Helios + 50 亿美元战略合作
AMD and Anthropic Announce 2GW Helios Deployment + $5B Strategic Investment
🆕 US
7 月 22 日，AMD 与 Anthropic 宣布建立战略合作伙伴关系：Anthropic 将在 AMD Helios 机架级解决方案中部署最高达 2 吉瓦的 AMD Instinct MI450 系列 GPU（首批 1GW 计划于 2027 年上半年启动），采用 MI455X GPU、EPYC "Venice" CPU、Pensando 网络与 ROCm 软件栈。AMD 承诺未来向 Anthropic 进行最高 50 亿美元（约 339 亿元人民币）的战略股权投资——这是 AMD 首次投资 Anthropic。双方还将启动多年工程合作：用 Claude 优化面向 AMD Instinct GPU 的工作负载并加速 ROCm 发展，AMD 亦将在工程与产品开发团队中广泛采用 Claude。该合作建立在 Anthropic 已采用 MI355X GPU 的基础之上。
On July 22, AMD and Anthropic announced a strategic partnership: Anthropic will deploy up to 2 gigawatts of AMD Instinct MI450-series GPUs in AMD Helios rack-scale solutions (first 1GW targeted for H1 2027), using MI455X GPUs, EPYC "Venice" CPUs, Pensando networking, and the ROCm software stack. AMD committed up to $5 billion (~¥33.9B RMB) in strategic equity investment in Anthropic — AMD's first investment in the company. The pair also launched a multi-year engineering collaboration: using Claude to optimize workloads for AMD Instinct GPUs and accelerate ROCm, while AMD broadly adopts Claude across its engineering and product teams. The deal builds on Anthropic's existing use of MI355X GPUs.
为何重要：这是 Anthropic 首次大规模采用非英伟达硬件，标志着前沿模型训练/推理的「硬件多元化」从口号走向吉瓦级合同。对 AMD 而言，Helios 机架方案获得 Claude 这一顶级工作负载背书，直接挑战英伟达 Vera Rubin 生态。50 亿美元股权绑定也复制了 AMD 与 Meta 的合作模式（Meta 计划部署至多 6GW AMD 芯片）。
Why it matters:This is Anthropic's first large-scale adoption of non-Nvidia hardware, moving frontier training/inference "hardware diversification" from rhetoric to gigawatt-scale contracts. For AMD, Helios gains backing from a top-tier workload (Claude), directly challenging Nvidia's Vera Rubin ecosystem. The $5B equity tie also mirrors AMD's Meta playbook (Meta plans up to 6GW of AMD chips).

谷歌研发 Frozen v2 芯片：将 Gemini 架构固化进硅片
Google Developing "Frozen v2" Chip That Bakes Gemini Architecture Into Silicon
US
据 The Information 7 月 20 日报道，谷歌正在研发代号 "Frozen v2" 的服务器芯片，将 Gemini 大模型的底层神经网络架构（层级结构、注意力机制、残差连接）在芯片设计阶段直接固化进物理电路，而非仅加载权重。工程师预计其单位功耗 token 处理量可达现有 TPU 的 6–10 倍，目标部署时间 2028 年。该芯片独立于 TPU 产品线、不取代后者，是针对 Gemini 的专用 ASIC 分支。初代方案由 Jeff Dean 牵头曾试图固化完整模型权重，因硬件生命周期过短被搁置；Frozen v2 改为「冻结架构、权重可更新」，押注 Gemini 底层架构已稳定到值得用硅片记录。谷歌发言人回应称「并非所有项目都会进入量产」，但强调严格探索是全栈战略的核心。
Per a July 20 report from The Information, Google is developing a server chip codenamed "Frozen v2" that etches Gemini's underlying neural architecture (layer structure, attention, residual connections) directly into physical circuits at design time, rather than merely loading weights. Engineers project 6–10x more tokens per watt than current TPUs, with deployment targeted for 2028. The chip sits alongside — not replacing — the TPU line as a Gemini-specific ASIC branch. An earlier Jeff Dean-led design tried to bake in full weights but was shelved for too-short hardware lifespan; Frozen v2 "freezes the architecture, keeps weights updatable," betting Gemini's base architecture is now stable enough to record in silicon. Google said "not every project moves to production" but emphasized rigorous exploration is central to its full-stack approach.
为何重要：Frozen v2 代表 AI 基础设施竞争规则的改变——芯片开始为单一模型定制，放弃通用性换取极致能效。前提是模型架构已稳定到值得「硅化」。若 2028 年量产，将从根本上缓解谷歌内部算力短缺（据称已迫使谷歌云拒绝部分外部客户），并可能催生「模型架构 ASIC」这一新品类。
Why it matters:Frozen v2 signals a shift in AI-infrastructure rules — chips are now customized for a single model, trading generality for extreme efficiency. The premise is that model architectures have stabilized enough to "siliconize." If it ships in 2028, it would ease Google's internal compute shortage (reportedly forcing Cloud to turn down outside customers) and could spawn a "model-architecture ASIC" category.

Alibaba Releases Qwen-Image-3.0 Image Generation Foundation Model

The model accepts up to 4.5k-token prompts to compose information-dense images (infographics, nested UI mockups) in one pass, renders text as small as 10 pixels with photorealistic textures, and natively supports 12 languages plus high-fidelity simulation of mainstream web and game interfaces. Why it matters:Qwen-Image-3.0 pushes image generation to "layout-level" precision (4.5k-token input, 10px text), but continues Alibaba's recent launch style — no benchmarks, parameters, license, or technical report, only cherry-picked examples. Unlike open-weight 1.0/2.0 (Apache 2.0), 3.0 is not yet open, testing developer trust.

Kimi K3 Released: 2.8T Frontier-Class Open Model, Weights To Open July 27
CN
Moonshot AI 于 7 月 16 日发布 Kimi K3——2800 亿（2.8T）参数的稀疏混合专家模型，号称全球首个 3T 级开放模型。模型每 token 仅激活 896 个专家中的 16 个（约 1.8%），配备 100 万 token 上下文、原生视觉理解与常开「思考模式」，采用 MXFP4 权重 + MXFP8 激活的量化感知训练。在 GDPval-AA v2 基准上得分 1687，仅次于 Claude Fable 5 Max（1815）与 GPT-5.6 Sol Max（1747.8），超越 Claude Opus 4.8（1600）；BrowseComp 达 91.2 居全球第一。API 已上线，完整权重定于 7 月 27 日开放，采用开源权重许可（许可证随权重发布确定）。Artificial Analysis 将其智能指数列为全球第三。
Moonshot AI released Kimi K3 on July 16 — a 2.8-trillion-parameter sparse MiE model billed as the world's first 3T-class open model. It activates only 16 of 896 experts per token (~1.8%), with a 1M-token context, native vision, always-on "thinking mode," and MXFP4-weight / MXFP8-activation quantization-aware training. On GDPval-AA v2 it scored 1,687 — behind only Claude Fable 5 Max (1,815) and GPT-5.6 Sol Max (1,747.8), ahead of Claude Opus 4.8 (1,600); it leads globally on BrowseComp at 91.2. 为何重要：Kimi K3 将开放模型与闭源前沿的差距从此前常说的 6–9 个月缩短到 3–5 个月，且以更低价格（输出 100 元/百万 token）冲击 GPT 与 Claude 的调用量。其架构创新（Kimi Delta Attention、Attention Residuals）已作为开放研究发布。权重开放后将使全球开发者首次获得万亿级参数的可自托管前沿基座。
Why it matters:Kimi K3 narrows the open-vs-closed frontier gap from the often-cited 6–9 months to 3–5 months, and at lower price (¥100/M output tokens) pressures GPT and Claude call volume. Its architectural innovations (Kimi Delta Attention, Attention Residuals) are already published as open research. Once weights drop, developers gain their first self-hostable frontier-class base at the trillion-parameter scale.

DeepMind《GenCeption》：视频生成模型即通用视觉学习器
DeepMind "GenCeption": Video Generation Models Are General-Purpose Vision Learners
Research
DeepMind（Kaiming He、Andrew Zisserman 等参与）的 ECCV 2026 论文《Video Generation Models are General-Purpose Vision Learners》提出 GenCeption：将预训练文生视频扩散模型改造为统一的单步前馈通用视觉感知模型，用统一的 L2 损失在合成数据上联合训练深度、法线、分割、指代表达分割、相机位姿与 3D 关键点等多任务。实验显示其在多个基准上接近或超越 DepthAnything3、SAM3、D4RT、VGGT-Ω、Sapiens 等专用模型，且仅用约 123 万帧后训练数据即接近部分领先专用模型；生成式预训练主干（WAN 2.1）在深度估计上明显优于 V-JEPA、VideoMAE V2。模型还展现出 sim-to-real 迁移与对分布外类别的泛化等涌现行为。
An ECCV 2026 paper from DeepMind (with Kaiming He and Andrew Zisserman) titled "Video Generation Models are General-Purpose Vision Learners" introduces GenCeption: repurposing a pre-trained text-to-video diffusion model into a unified single-step feed-forward generalist vision model, jointly trained on synthetic data with a unified L2 loss across depth, normals, segmentation, referring segmentation, camera pose, and 3D keypoints. It matches or surpasses specialist models (DepthAnything3, SAM3, D4RT, VGGT-Ω, Sapiens) on multiple benchmarks, reaches comparable performance with only ~1.23M post-training frames, and its generative-pretrained backbone (WAN 2.1) beats V-JEPA and VideoMAE V2 on depth estimation. It also shows emergent sim-to-real transfer and out-of-distribution generalization.
为何重要：论文主张「大规模文生视频生成」是计算机视觉的通用预训练范式——类比 NLP 的下一词元预测，为视觉领域提供时空先验与视觉-语言对齐。若成立，将推动 CV 从「专用模型时代」走向「通用基础模型时代」，并解释为何视频生成值得产业重金投入。
Why it matters:The paper argues large-scale text-to-video generation is the general pre-training paradigm for vision — analogous to next-token prediction in NLP — supplying spatiotemporal priors and vision-language alignment. If validated, it would move CV from a "specialist-model era" to a "generalist-foundation-model era" and explain why video generation deserves heavy industrial investment.

Hugging Face LeRobot v0.6.0：世界模型与奖励 API 闭合机器人学习闭环
Hugging Face LeRobot v0.6.0: World Models & Reward API Close the Robot-Learning Loop
Research
Hugging Face LeRobot 团队发布 v0.6.0（主题 "Imagine, Evaluate, Improve"），将机器人学习的完整闭环——策略学习、成功评估、失败纠正、云端训练——整合进单一可复现工具链。核心更新包括：三类世界模型策略（VLA-JEPA、FastWAM、LingBot-VA），让机器人在行动前「想象」未来轨迹以减少复合误差；标准化奖励模型 API（Robometer、TOPReward）自动判断任务成败，免去人工标注瓶颈；统一评测 CLI（lerobot-eval）整合 6 个仿真基准（含 LIBERO、MetaWorld）；DAgger 式人在环纠正可经 HF Jobs 将部署失败直接转为新训练数据；并修复 CVE-2026-25874（pickle 反序列化 RCE）。安装体积精简约 40% 依赖，支持 PyTorch 2.7+、FSDP 与云端训练。
The Hugging Face LeRobot team released v0.6.0 ("Imagine, Evaluate, Improve"), pulling the full robot-learning loop — policy learning, success evaluation, failure correction, cloud training — into a single reproducible toolchain. Highlights: three world-model policies (VLA-JEPA, FastWAM, LingBot-VA) that "imagine" future trajectories before acting to cut compounding error; a standardized reward-model API (Robometer, TOPReward) that auto-judges task success, removing the human-labeling bottleneck; a unified lerobot-eval CLI across six simulation benchmarks (LIBERO, MetaWorld); DAgger-style human-in-the-loop correction that routes deployment failures into HF Jobs as new training data; and a fix for CVE-2026-25874 (pickle deserialization RCE). Install footprint is ~40% leaner, with PyTorch 2.7+, FSDP, and cloud training support.
为何重要：LeRobot v0.6.0 把「评估与纠正」提升为与策略训练同等的一等公民，将具身智能的竞争框架从「谁的 demo 更顺滑」转向「谁的系统迭代失败最快」。对无力自建基础设施的小团队与学术实验室，这是严肃机器人学习研究的可靠起点。
Why it matters:LeRobot v0.6.0 elevates evaluation and correction to first-class citizens alongside policy training, shifting embodied-AI competition from "whose demo looks smoothest" to "whose system iterates on failures fastest." For small teams and academic labs without bespoke infrastructure, it is a credible starting point for serious robot-learning research.

Kimi K3 分裂美国 AI 政策圈，白宫据报重新评估限制中国开放模型
Kimi K3 Splits US AI Policy Circles; White House Reportedly Reconsiders Curbs on Chinese Open Models
Policy
MIT Technology Review 7 月 21 日报道，Kimi K3 的发布使特朗普政府内部围绕「如何应对中国免费开放模型」爆发公开分裂。Axios 7 月 20 日称，白宫正重新讨论是否限制中国先进 AI 模型进入美国市场，可能路径包括政府采购规定、实体清单威胁与公开施压，而非全面禁令。前 AI 与加密事务负责人 David Sacks 在 X 上批评头部闭源实验室「想借政府之手清除开源竞争对手」，而五角大楼官员 Emil Michael 则主张将中国开放模型视为需立法管控的国家安全威胁。报道还称微软正在评估将 Kimi K3 接入 Azure 并测试其能否接替部分 GPT/Claude 任务。Nathan Lambert 估算开放模型与闭源前沿差距已缩至 3–5 个月。
MIT Technology Review reported July 21 that Kimi K3's launch triggered an open split inside the Trump administration over how to counter China's free open models. Axios said July 20 the White House is re-discussing limits on Chinese advanced AI models entering the US, likely via procurement rules, entity-list threats, and public pressure rather than an outright ban. Former AI/crypto czar David Sacks argued on X that leading closed labs "want the government to eliminate their open-source competition," while Pentagon official Emil Michael framed Chinese open models as a national-security threat needing legislative control. 为何重要：这是「开放模型」首次成为美国大国博弈的核心议题。若限制落地，将重塑全球 AI 供应链与企业采用逻辑，并可能反向刺激中国加强自身模型与芯片出口管控（FT 报道北京正考虑相关措施）。开放 vs 封闭的路线之争正从技术圈外溢为地缘政治。
