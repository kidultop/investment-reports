S&P 500 Closes Out Its Worst Week Since the Iran War Started
标普500本周跌幅创伊朗战争爆发以来最差，连续五周收跌
The S&P 500 fell 1.7% to close at 6,368.85 points on Friday, marking the index's worst week since the Iran conflict began and its fifth consecutive weekly decline -- the longest losing streak in nearly four years. 标普500周五下跌1.7%收于6368.85点，标志着自伊朗战争以来最差单周表现，同时也是连续第五周收跌，为近四年最长连跌周期。亚马逊、Meta、英伟达等大型科技股均告下跌。10年期美债收益率升至4.43%，VIX恐慌指数飙升至31以上。

EU Warns of Stagflation Risk as Iran War Pushes Energy Prices Higher
欧盟警告面临滞胀风险，伊朗战争推高能源成本
EU economic officials warned Thursday that Europe faces a real risk of a stagflationary shock from the ongoing conflict. The EU estimates the war could trim GDP growth by 0.4 percentage points this year, and by 0.6 points in both 2026 and 2027 if the conflict persists. European stocks also closed lower on Friday despite Trump's latest delay.
欧盟经济官员周四警告，欧洲面临持续冲突带来的真实滞胀性冲击风险。欧盟估计，战争可能令今年GDP增长减少0.4个百分点，若冲突持续，2026和2027年增长将各减少0.6个百分点。欧洲股市周五也在特朗普最新延期消息下低收。

Stocks Fall, Oil Prices Rise After Trump Extends Iran Deadline to April 6
特朗普将伊朗最后期限延至4月6日，股市下跌、油价攀升
US stocks tumbled on Friday, capping the worst week since the Iran war began. The S&P 500 fell 1.7%, the Dow Jones dropped 793 points, and the Nasdaq lost 2.1%. Trump extended the deadline for Iran to open the Strait of Hormuz to April 6, but the move failed to reassure investors. Brent crude rose 3.4% to $105.32/barrel while WTI surged 5.5% to $99.64/barrel.
美股周五大幅下跌，本周成为伊朗战争爆发以来最糟糕的一周。标普500跌1.7%，道琼斯大跌793点，纳斯达克跌2.1%。特朗普将伊朗开放霍尔木兹海峡的最后期限延至4月6日，但未能安抚投资者情绪。布伦特原油涨3.4%至105.32美元/桶，WTI原油涨5.5%至99.64美元/桶。

Bank of Japan Releases Updated Neutral Rate Estimate, Little Impact on Policy Path
日本央行发布最新中性利率区间估计，对政策路径影响有限
The Bank of Japan on Friday released an updated estimate of Japan's natural rate of interest, showing a range of around -0.9% to +0.5%. Analysts said the estimate is unlikely to change expectations for BoJ monetary policy. The BoJ has signaled a hawkish bias due to Iran war-driven inflation, maintaining its key rate at 0.75%. Japan's 10-year bond yield rose to 2.38%.
日本央行周五发布中性利率最新估计区间，约为-0.9%至+0.5%，与此前预测差异不大。分析师认为该估计不太可能改变对日银政策路径的预期。日银维持基准利率0.75%不变，但因伊朗战争驱动通胀而发出鹰派信号。日本10年期国债收益率升至2.38%。
