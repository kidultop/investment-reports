OpenAI 升级 ChatGPT 安全对话机制（5月14日）
OpenAI Enhances Sensitive Conversation Safety in ChatGPT
OpenAI 于5月14日发布 ChatGPT 安全对话改进，引入「安全摘要」（Safety Summaries）机制——短小、事实性、有时间限制的先前安全相关上下文注释，仅用于高风险场景（自杀、自残、伤害他人）。在长对话中，自杀/自残案例安全响应提升50%，伤害他人案例提升16%；在 GPT-5.5 Instant 默认模型上，伤害他人场景提升52%，自杀/自残场景提升39%。与 OpenAI 全球医生网络心理健康专家合作开发。
OpenAI released safety improvements for ChatGPT on May 14, introducing Safety Summaries — short, factual, time-limited notes of prior safety-relevant context used only for high-risk scenarios (suicide, self-harm, harm to others). Safe-response performance improved 50% for suicide/self-harm and 16% for harm-to-others in long single conversations; 52% (harm-to-others) and 39% (suicide/self-harm) on the GPT-5.5 Instant default model. Developed with input from mental health experts in OpenAI's Global Physicians Network.
为何重要：这是大模型安全领域的重要工程突破，首次将长期上下文安全机制工程化落地。
Why it matters: An important engineering breakthrough in LLM safety — the first time long-context safety mechanisms have been industrialized.
