OpenAI Launches GPT-Live Full-Duplex Voice Models, Making Voice the Unified ChatGPT Interface
7 月 8 日，OpenAI 正式发布 GPT-Live 系列语音模型（GPT-Live-1 及 GPT-Live-1 mini），采用原生全双工架构，可同时聆听与说话，支持打断、停顿理解、实时翻译与后台推理。模型在对话中能以"嗯哼"等短语表明正在倾听，遇到复杂任务时自动调用 GPT-5.5 处理推理与搜索，并在后台完成后无缝返回结果。每周超过 1.5 亿人使用 ChatGPT 语音功能。
On July 8, OpenAI released GPT-Live-1 and GPT-Live-1 mini, voice models built on a native full-duplex architecture that can listen and speak simultaneously. The models support interruption, pause comprehension, live translation, and background reasoning delegation. When complex tasks arise, GPT-Live delegates to GPT-5.5 for search and reasoning, seamlessly returning results while keeping the conversation flowing. Over 150M people use ChatGPT Voice weekly.
为何重要：这是语音 AI 交互的范式跃升——从轮流对话到全双工实时交互，语音不再只是"输入方式"而成为 AI 系统的统一入口。后台委托机制使语音可以同时处理简单对话和复杂推理，为 agentic 语音助手奠定架构基础。
Why it matters:A paradigm leap in voice AI interaction — from turn-based to full-duplex real-time, voice is no longer just an "input method" but the unified interface for AI systems. The delegation architecture enables simultaneous handling of casual conversation and complex reasoning, laying the foundation for agentic voice assistants.
